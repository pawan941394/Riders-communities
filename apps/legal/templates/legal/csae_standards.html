Child Safety and CSAE Standards

{{ site_name }} · Last updated: {{ last_updated }}

{{ operator_name }} has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We prohibit any content, behavior, or account activity related to child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexual exploitation of minors, or any attempt to facilitate such harm.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement immediately.

1. Prohibited content and behavior

  • Any CSAM, including images, videos, links, or text descriptions.
  • Sexualization of minors or any exploitative behavior involving minors.
  • Grooming, solicitation, trafficking, coercion, or attempts to exploit minors.
  • Sharing instructions or services that enable CSAE activity.

2. Detection and enforcement

We may use a combination of user reports, automated checks, and manual review to detect violations. Accounts or content that violate these standards may be removed immediately. We may suspend or permanently ban accounts and preserve evidence where required by law.

3. Reporting

Users can report abusive or suspicious behavior through in-app help/contact channels. We review high-risk reports with priority and act quickly to reduce harm.

4. Cooperation with authorities

Where legally required, we cooperate with relevant law enforcement and child safety authorities, including reporting CSAM and preserving required records.

5. Child safety commitment

We continuously improve our trust and safety controls, moderation workflows, and escalation procedures to prevent abuse and protect users, especially children.
